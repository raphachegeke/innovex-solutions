NEED HELP?

Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about
our services, therapy, and mental well
being.

How do I book a therapy session?
You can book a session through our online portal or contact support for assistance.
Are online sessions available?
Yes, we offer online video and chat sessions for convenience.
What’s the difference between therapy and coaching?
Therapy focuses on mental health and emotional healing, while coaching is goal-oriented and future-focused.
Do I need a subscription to access services?
Some services require a subscription, while others are pay-as-you-go.
Can I switch therapists if I don’t feel the right connection?
Yes, you're free to switch therapists to find the right fit.
Is my information and session history kept confidential?
Absolutely. We follow strict confidentiality policies to protect your data.
